The Weeknd is the stage name of a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer whose real name is Abel Tesfaye. He is known for his distinctive style of music, which combines elements of R&B, pop, and electronic music. The Weeknd has gained widespread recognition for his hit songs and albums and has been a prominent figure in the music industry.
